A competitive programming analytics platform that aggregates student activity across LeetCode, GitHub, Codeforces, CodeChef, and HackerRank into a single dashboard. Features batch-scoped leaderboards so students rank within their class — not against millions globally — boosting morale and visibility. SEWA

SEWA (Sepsis Early Warning Agent) is a real-time clinical decision-support system built for Intensive Care Units (ICUs). The system monitors continuous patient vital signs and intermittent laboratory results, applies rule-based screening (SIRS, qSOFA) and an ML-based risk model (LSTM + XGBoost), and delivers explainable, auditable alerts to clinical staff — enabling earlier intervention and reducing sepsis mortality
